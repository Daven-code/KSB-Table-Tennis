About Us

KSB Table Tennis Club is a community of around 120 young and adult players, parents and friends connected to Kay Street Baptist Church. Founded in 1991 with just 4 players, we are now the largest club in the East Lancs Table Tennis League with 6 junior and 7 adult teams.

Although we have been Premier Division champions for the past 6 years the Club fosters a friendly, sportsmanship and incluse environment where players of all ages and skill levels can enjoy the sport. We have a development coaching programme which takes young players who are absolute beginners to a point where they are able to represent the Club by playing in the league. During the closed season (April to September) we have open Club Nights every Tuesday and Wednesday to which new players are welcome.
