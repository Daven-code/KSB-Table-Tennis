About Us

KSB Table Tennis Club is a community of around 120 players of all ages, together with parents and friends connected to Kay Street Baptist Church. Founded in 1991 with just four members, the club has grown to become the largest in the East Lancashire Table Tennis League, with six junior teams and seven adult teams.

Although we have been Premier Division champions for the past six years, the club is committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment where players of all ages and abilities can enjoy the sport. Our development coaching programme supports young beginners and helps them progress to the level where they can represent the club in league competitions. During the closed season (April to September), we run open club nights every Tuesday and Wednesday, which new players are warmly invited to attend.
